How It Works

Everything you need to know about Morti's Masters Pool 2026.

⛳

Pick Your Roster

Each entry: 4 Core golfers + 1 Alternate. Pick any golfer in the Masters field. Give your entry a team name — that's what shows on the leaderboard and what you use as your payment memo. Picks are completely private — other participants only see team names and prize totals.

✂️

The Cut & Alternate Rule

  • If one starter misses the cut, your alternate takes their spot.
  • If more than one starter misses the cut, you play shorthanded — the alternate only covers one slot. The rest count as $0 (missed-cut payment of $25,000 each).
  • If all four starters make the cut, your alternate is Mr. Irrelevant and contributes nothing to your total.
  • If you don't specify an alternate, the last golfer on your list becomes the alternate.
💰

Prize Money Scoring & Pot Payout

Your score is the combined projected prize money of your active golfers based on their leaderboard position. Projected live using the 2025 Masters payout structure ($21M purse). Missed cut = $25,000.

Tiebreaker

If two or more entries are tied on prize money, the tiebreaker is whoever's guess is closest to Brian Harman's final score relative to par.

• If Brian makes the cut: his 72-hole total to par.
• If Brian misses the cut: his 36-hole total after Round 2.

The closest guess wins — regardless of whether it's over or under. If Brian finishes -8 and one person guessed -10 and another guessed -6, both are equally close (2 strokes away) and they split the pot for that place. There is no "closest without going over" rule — pure proximity wins.
